Tales of a Dreamer

               My
            mind
           would
          never feel
        the sun. Never
       feel its hot
      embers infiltrate
     my unoccupied
   space.Only
nightmares
came, cold,longing,dark
Making a home in my
bed,But its unsympathetic
embrace, would only soon
captivate me And I would
    call back Longing,
      Craving, Dreaming

- Tijorn Skelton
